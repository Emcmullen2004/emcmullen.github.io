HTML Basics


The first think we learned about was the "p" tag, it is used to write text in your website, to use it you type:
<p>write your text here </p> in your body tag.


Next we learned about how to add links which was the "a" tag. To use it you type:
<a href="paste your link here">type the text that shows as the link here</a>. This goes in your body tag as well.


The last thing we learned was headings which is represented by the "h" tag. headings consist of 1 - 6, the lower the number the bigger the text. To use it you type:
<h1, 2 ,3 ,4 ,5 or 6>Type your heading here</h1, 2, 3, 4, ,5 or 6>
