IFrames!

Iframes are made to access a webiste inside another website, web-ception! They're how people add youtube videos into websites.
In fact your reading this through an IFrame. The way to use an iframe is:
<iframe src="add your link here" frameborder="define if you want a frame border here with 1 or 0"
Width="define the length of your iframe in pixels" height="define how tall your iframe is in pixels"><iframe>


P.S, if you want to be extra fancy you can move the link (in "src") to an "a" tag and have the iframe have a name tag.
In the anchor tag(a) add a target, in the target atribute should be the name of your iframe. Now once you click the link it will display the iframe.
